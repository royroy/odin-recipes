Shavocado! the legend

This is the ultimate drunken meal, it even includes beer!

Ingredients


  • Beer (obviously)
  • 3 sausages, your choice of flavor or type
  • 1 each, red green and yellow or orange pepper
  • one large onion
  • 4-5 large potatoes
  • Milk
  • butter
  • salt, pepper, old bay, smoked paprika

Steps

  1. on medium high heat, sear the sausages and set aside
  2. reduce heat to medium, add peppers and onions
  3. once softened up half way, slowly add beer and scrape up the brown bits from
    the bottom of the pan add back sausages and cook for 15-20 mins depending on preferance
  4. serve over mashed potatoes using the liquid as a gravy
