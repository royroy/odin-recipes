Grilled Veggies


This delicious dish is deceptively simple and quick to make

ingredients

  • Various vegetables of your choice
  • High smoke point oil such as canola
  • Vegeta seasoning

Steps

  1. cut and wash vegetable and place in a large ziplock
  2. Add 3 tablespoons of oil
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of vegeta seasoning
  4. close bag and shake, marinate for about 1 hr
  5. bbq over medium heat until desired doneness and enjoy!
